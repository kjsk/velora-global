## Option 1: Render.com (FREE)
- **Pros**: Great for Node.js, free tier, easy deployment
- **Cons**: Free instances sleep after 15 minutes (slow first load)
- **URL**: https://render.com

## Option 2: Vercel (FREE)
- **Pros**: Can host both frontend AND backend together
- **Cons**: Backend functions have time limits
- **URL**: https://vercel.com

## Option 3: Heroku (FREE)
- **Pros**: Classic platform, good documentation
- **Cons**: Free tier sleeps after 30 minutes
- **URL**: https://heroku.com

## Option 4: GoDaddy VPS (PAID)
- **Pros**: Full control, can host everything
- **Cons**: More expensive, requires server management
- **Price**: ~$5-20/month

## Option 5: Netlify Functions (FREE)
- **Pros**: Serverless functions, good for APIs
- **Cons**: Function execution limits
- **URL**: https://netlify.com

## üéØ My Recommendation
**Start with Railway** - it's the most reliable free option that won't sleep your backend.
